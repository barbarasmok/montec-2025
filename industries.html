01

Sanitary Market

We specialize in large-scale sanitary projects, working with the country’s main companies to ensure access to essential basic services. Our experience includes the construction of wastewater treatment and purification plants, the installation and rehabilitation of potable water and sewage networks, and the implementation of stormwater collector systems. We focus on resource optimization and the use of innovative technologies to reduce environmental impact and improve the efficiency of every project.

In addition to traditional potable water pipeline works, Montec introduced HDPE pipes to replace or recover old concrete, asbestos cement, and cast iron pipes using the "cracking" method—a term used by Montec to describe the trenchless system of introducing HDPE pipes into existing pipelines without open-pit excavation.

Intake systems are structures designed to capture the water required for processes, while outfall systems allow for the controlled discharge of the effluents generated during those processes.

Wastewater treatment plants—preliminary, primary, and secondary treatment—have been a recurring part of our construction development. We have built potable water treatment plants in major urban areas, such as the Padre Hurtado Potable Water Plant in the commune of La Reina, with a capacity of 2,500 liters per second.

Our electromechanical assembly services cover a wide range of applications, from industrial and commercial installations to infrastructure projects. Our team of engineering and construction experts is equipped to carry out electromechanical assembly projects of any scale and complexity. We work closely with our clients to understand their needs and provide solutions that meet the most demanding standards.

Our industrial assembly services span a wide range of applications, from machinery and equipment installation to the construction of complete industrial plants. We work in close collaboration with our clients, understanding their requirements and adapting to their specific needs.

Our earthmoving services cover a broad range of activities, including excavation, site leveling, debris removal, base preparation, embankment construction, and more. We work closely with our clients to understand their specific needs and deliver tailored solutions.

We manage the entire project process, from conceptualization to final commissioning. We work in close collaboration with our clients to understand their specific requirements and deliver customized solutions that align with their needs and goals.

Water pumping systems require large pumping stations, powered by high-pressure pumps that exceed 1,500 HP, with up to 8 pumps per station.

We take pride in offering specialized services in submarine outfalls. With deep expertise in this area, our engineering and construction team is dedicated to designing and building efficient and reliable submarine outfalls, ensuring proper and sustainable effluent management.

Our network maintenance services cover a wide range of activities, including inspection, cleaning, repair, and preventive maintenance. We work closely with government agencies, companies, and communities, delivering tailored solutions that meet the specific needs of each project.

Our technical and operational capabilities allow us to tackle highly complex projects under extreme conditions, ensuring the efficiency and durability of each work.
