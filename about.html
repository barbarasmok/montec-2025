About Us

Leading Innovation in Engineering and Construction

Building the future with innovation and experience

MONTEC is a leading company in the development and execution of industrial, sanitary, and civil works. With over 30 years of experience, we have specialized in fluid transport projects, submarine outfalls, water treatment plants, industrial process plants, and wastewater sanitation. Our commitment to the highest standards of quality and safety has positioned us as a reliable partner for the industry’s most demanding clients.

Our Mission

Excellence in Service to the Community

At MONTEC, our mission is to place the talent of our professionals at the service of the community through the development of projects and the execution of sustainable work. We strive to be a trustworthy company where clients can delegate the full scope of work with satisfactory results.

Our History

Since 1994

With over 30 years of experience, Montec has established itself as a leader in the development and execution of industrial, sanitary, and civil works. Our history is marked by the execution of projects such as fluid transport, submarine outfalls, water treatment plants, industrial process plants, and wastewater sanitation.

Over the years, we have invested in cutting-edge technology and professional machinery, now owning more than 3,000 assets. This constant advancement and continuous improvement have driven us toward business excellence, allowing us to shift market standards and offer innovative solutions to our clients.
